# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the ForecastIQ community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:
- Harassment, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project
maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
